Overview

This guide will walk you through the daily routine of managing your boutique's register. It is required to start your day here before generating any sales invoices, and to end your day here to reconcile your collections.

Section 1 — Getting Started & Dashboard Overview

1.1 Reading the Status Dashboard

Daily Check

Objective

Learn how to access your workspace and instantly know the status of your boutique's register based on the Smart Status Card.

Understanding the Colors

🟠 Orange (Pending Start): Your shift hasn't been started today. You need to open the register.

🟢 Green (Active Shift): Your register is open and running. You are ready to process sales.

🔴 Red (Action Required): You forgot to close a previous shift. You are locked out of today until you close the missed day.

🟣 Purple (Completed): You have successfully ended today's shift and reconciled all cash.

Access Denied?

If you see a grey "Access Denied / No Boutique Assigned" screen with a lock icon, it means your user profile hasn't been linked to a specific store yet. Please contact your system administrator to assign you to your boutique.

Section 2 — Starting Your Day

2.1 Opening the Register

Morning Task

Objective

Officially open your register for the day. Note: You cannot create any Sales Invoices until this step is completed.

Steps to Start

  1. Click the button on your Orange dashboard card to go to the Day Entry page.
  2. Review the Opening Petty Cash. The system automatically fills this with whatever cash you left in the drawer yesterday.
  3. If the actual cash in the drawer is different, adjust the number manually.
  4. Add any Remarks (optional morning notes).
  5. Click the Start Day → button.

Expected Result

A green success message will appear. The screen will update to show your "Day End" form, and your workspace dashboard will turn Green (Active). You can now process sales normally.

Section 3 — Ending Your Day

3.1 Closing the Register & Collections

Evening Task

Objective

Log daily footfall, enter payment collections, attach settlement proofs, and officially close the shift.

Steps to Close

  1. Navigate back to the Day Entry page at the end of your shift.
  2. Customer Count: Enter the total number of Walk-ins, New Customers, and Existing Customers.
  3. Invoices: Enter the total number of invoices generated.
    💡 Smart Alert: If the number you type doesn't match the actual number of invoices in the system, it will highlight in red.
  4. Payments: Enter the total collections for Cash, Credit Card, Bank Transfer, and Other.
  5. Attach Proof: Under the payment fields, click "Attach Files" to upload your daily EDC machine settlement receipts or bank screenshots.
  6. Closing Petty Cash: Enter the final amount of physical cash left in the drawer. (This will be tomorrow's opening cash).
  7. Click End Day →.

Important Note

Once you click "End Day", the entry is locked and cannot be edited. Ensure your totals match before submitting.

Section 4 — Handling Missed Entries

4.1 Resolving Previous Days

⚠️ Troubleshooting

What Happens if I Forget to Close?

If you forget to end your day yesterday, the system will lock your register today. Your dashboard will turn Red, and you will not be able to start a new shift.

How to Fix It

  1. Click the button on the Red dashboard card to go to the Day Entry page.
  2. You will see a red "Previous Day" warning at the top of the form, showing the date you missed.
  3. Fill out the customer counts, payments, and closing cash exactly as you would for a normal day close.
  4. Click End Day.
  5. The system will now unlock, and present you with today's "Start Day" form so you can continue working normally.

Section 5 — Viewing History

5.1 Past Entries & Summaries

Data Management

The "All Caught Up" Screen

Once you successfully close your register for the day, you will see the "All Caught Up!" screen. This provides a quick summary of your completed shift, including total hours worked, walk-ins, and collections breakdown.

Viewing Previous Days

  1. Scroll down past the main form to find the Previous Entries section.
  2. This list displays your last 7 closed shifts, along with their total collections and the time they were ended.
  3. Click on any date (or the ▼ arrow) to expand it.
  4. This will reveal the exact breakdown of Customer Counts, Payments, Remarks, and any attached files for that specific day.
  5. Click it again to collapse it.
